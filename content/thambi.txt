Title: Thambi
Description: Thambi is an upcoming Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Jeethu Joseph, starring Karthi, Jyothika and Sathyaraj in the lead roles. The film has been produced by Viacom 18 along with Parallel Mind Productions. It is scheduled to release in December 2019
Director: Jeethu Joseph
Year: 2019
Cast:  Karthi, Jyotika, Sathyaraj
